But we know you have questions, so here are some answers for you:

1. Why, guys? WHY?

We have read & heard countless times: make sure your estimating is bullet-proof...for production, stretch goals, freight AND shipping. We've worked hard to do this, but we've received some late-breaking competitive estimates that we want to investigate thoroughly to validate we're getting the best value, highest quality and most reliable service across the board.

We also feel strongly that we can't possibly over-prepare, so while we're working on buttoning-up numbers, we're also taking the extra time to further hone our full campaign plan (updates, social presence, etc) as well as continuing work on designing our new components and adding even more flare (ahem) to our game board.

2. So when ARE you launching?

Great question. We aren't going to pinpoint a date until we nail down that critical data we just mentioned, but we are committed to launching within the next 4-7 weeks. We will send another pre-launch announcement as we narrow-in on a new date.

3. What if I just can't wait anymore?

We know it's difficult...it's hard for us too. We're also working to get Sol up on Tabletop Simulator so very soon you will be able to play Sol with people all over the world!

4. I have other questions!

Thanks for showing so much interest! We are more than happy to correspond with you 1:1 to discuss more details around our decision. Simply reply to this email and we will get back to you within 24 hours.
