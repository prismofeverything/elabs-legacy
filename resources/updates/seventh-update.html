New Board made of Fire!



Ever since we originally conceived of the game, we imagined the board as the Sun. From the very beginning, throughout every phase of design, this guiding principle, that we are playing a game on the Sun, has driven every decision we've made.

Now, that vision is a reality. Finally, a board that is actually on fire! You cannot get closer to the Sun unless you literally trigger a fusion reaction on the table.
