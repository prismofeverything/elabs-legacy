New Board made of Fire!


Ever since we originally conceived of the game, we imagined the board as the Sun. From the very beginning this guiding principle, that we are playing a game on the Sun, has driven every decision we've made.

Now, that vision is a reality. Finally, a board that is actually on fire! (Actual prototype). You cannot get closer to the Sun unless you literally trigger a fusion reaction on the table.

Also, check out those sweet pieces. Thanks Portal Dragon!

[Jodi disclaimer: the board is not actually fire. No solar systems were hurt in the making of this game board.]
