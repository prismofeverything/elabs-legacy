Kickstarter Launch Date: February 2nd!


Preparations for the Kickstarter campaign are in full swing. So many elements are in motion: we have review copies out to reviewers, people working on video (animation, sound, live action, how to play, etc etc), designing the page, sculpting pieces, developing art, getting production quotes... the list goes on and on. It turns out that beyond designing a game being a monumental task, creating a Kickstarter campaign is also a giant task!

That said, everything is coming together. Barring any unforeseen events, the Kickstarter campaign for Sol: Last Days of a Star is going live in two weeks, February 2nd, 2016! We would love to have you be part of the campaign, and help us make this project which has been so much a part of our lives the past two years a reality! Thanks to all of you who have already helped us get to this point. We couldn't do it without you.

We will send out one more update that day to let everyone know we are live, and you can follow our Facebook or Twitter page to keep abreast of developments as they arise!

What's new? Quick Start Rules!


Based on user feedback and playtesting, we have added a setup variant for Quick Start! This compresses play time while providing an interesting strategic placement decision right away. This is a great way to introduce the game to people and prepare them for a strategic opening in future games.
