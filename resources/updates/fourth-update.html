Art Revolution


One of the most exciting things in recent weeks has been a complete renovation of the art for Sol by our dear friend over at Arms and Anchors, Mr. Mark Dusckiewicz. He's really captured the dark and smouldering spirit of Sol in a dazzling manner. Our greatest thanks to you Mark for the inspiration and majesty you bring to the game!

You can find previews of all on our Facebook page and also Boardgamegeek. Let us know what you think!
