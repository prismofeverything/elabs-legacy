Sol was created by Sean and Ryan Spangler, two brothers who have been playing and making games together since childhood. Sol represents the first concerted effort to realize a lifelong dream of creating and publishing a game for the rest of the world.

The original idea for Sol came from a desire to make a game whose board was a giant image of the Sun. The rest followed naturally, and Sean and Ryan discovered they really do love the process of game design as one of the ultimate creative acts.
